About the CTF Challenge

The CyberHunt Capture the Flag (CTF) competition is designed to test your skills in cybersecurity and programming.

Participants will solve various challenges by submitting flags to earn points. Each challenge targets different aspects of cybersecurity.

Are you ready to test your skills and earn a certificate of completion? Join the challenge now!

How to Participate

1. Click on the "Start CTF" button to enter your name.

2. Once your name is entered, you'll be redirected to the CTF challenges page.

3. Solve the challenges by entering the correct flags.

4. Earn points for each correct submission!

5. Upon completing all challenges, a personalized certificate will be generated for you.
